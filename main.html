Movie Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from deep within the subconscious during dreams. He is offered a chance at redemption if he can successfully perform inception—planting an idea into someone’s mind—while navigating layered dream worlds and dangerous projections.

Main Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Elliot Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Ken Watanabe as Saito. Each actor brings depth and intensity to the complex narrative.

Favorite Quote

“You mustn’t be afraid to dream a little bigger, darling.” This quote perfectly captures the film’s theme of ambition, imagination, and pushing beyond perceived limits.
