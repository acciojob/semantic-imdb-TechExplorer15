Inception

A science-fiction thriller that explores dreams within dreams.

Actors Quotes
Inception follows Dom Cobb, a professional thief who steals information from the subconscious during dreams. He is offered a chance to regain his normal life if he can perform the impossible task of inception— planting an idea into someone’s mind.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Elliot Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Ken Watanabe as Saito.
